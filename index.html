Tag Your Ride, Find Your Bike

RideTag is a simple mobile application that helps you keep track of where you parked your bike or scooter. Pin your location, tag your ride and come back later without losing time.

Learn More

About RideTag

RideTag is designed for cyclists and micro‑mobility users who want a quick way to remember where they left their ride. By using your device’s GPS, RideTag marks your location and stores it securely on your phone. No account or sign‑up is required.

Key Features

Quick Tagging

With a single tap you can save your current location as a ride tag. Your tags remain on your device until you remove them.

Easy Navigation

When it’s time to return, RideTag provides step‑by‑step directions back to your tagged location.

Offline Friendly

Your tags are stored locally, so you can still access them even when you have no network connection.
