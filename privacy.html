Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how RideTag ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and stores information when you use our mobile application and this website.

Information We Collect

RideTag is designed to function without requiring you to create an account. We collect minimal personal information. Specifically:

  • Location Data. With your permission, RideTag uses your deviceâ€™s GPS to determine your current location when you create a ride tag. The coordinates are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.
  • Support Communications. If you contact us by email, we collect your email address and any information you include in your message in order to respond to your request.
  • Usage Data. We may collect anonymized analytics about how you interact with our app and website (such as pages visited or features used) to improve our services. This data does not identify you personally.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect only for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve the RideTag application and website.
  • To respond to your inquiries and support requests.
  • To analyze usage trends and improve user experience.

Data Storage and Sharing

RideTag stores your ride tags only on your device. We do not store personal location data on our servers. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

Your Choices

You can enable or disable location services in your device settings at any time. If location access is disabled, RideTag will not be able to create ride tags. You may also delete ride tags at any time within the app.
